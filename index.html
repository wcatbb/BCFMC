Learning & Performing Music

The Bayou City Federated Music Club promotes music learning. We are a member of the National Federation of Music Clubs and the Federation of Music Clubs. 500+ students participate in our festival each February and those that receive the highest grade are eligible to participate in the annual Texas State Music Festival.

Our Promise To You

We are passionate about teaching and believe that music learning is a lifelong journey. We believe music is a gift to be shared among all ages, skill levels, and learning abilities and we welcome students from all disciplines and backgrounds.
