Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

Bayou City Federated Music Club (BCFMC) was formed to promote the learning of music from American composers. The club is a member of the National Federation of Music Clubs and the Texas Federation of Music Clubs. The BCFMC has held its Music Festival annually in February where 500+ students participate. Students receiving the highest grade are eligible to participate in the State Music Festival held in May.

We guarantee that you will fall in lust with our decadent blends the moment you walk inside until you finish your last sip. Join us for your daily routine, an outing with friends, or simply just to enjoy some alone time.
